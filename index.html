Reconstitution: Add 2 mL bacteriostatic water to a 10 mg Tesamorelin vial. Gently swirl — do not shake. Result: 5 mg/mL.

Supplies Checklist

  • Insulin syringes (U-100, 1 mL, 31G): 60–90 per month.
  • 3 mL syringes with 25G needles: 8–12 per month — for Glutathione.
  • Alcohol swabs: 120+ per month (2 per injection).
  • Bacteriostatic water (30 mL): 1–2 bottles.
  • Sharps container: FDA-cleared, puncture-resistant.
  • Refrigerated storage: 2–8°C (36–46°F).
  • Glucose tablets: For IGF-1 LR3 hypoglycemia safety.
  • Benadryl (25–50 mg): For Tesamorelin histamine reaction backup.
  • Blood glucose monitor: During IGF-1 LR3 cycles.

Injection Technique

  1. Wash hands thoroughly.
  2. Wipe vial stopper with alcohol swab. Let dry.
  3. Draw air into syringe equal to dose. Inject air into vial.
  4. Draw dose slowly. Flick bubbles, expel air.
  5. Wipe injection site with fresh alcohol swab. Let dry.
  6. Pinch skin fold. Insert at 45° (lean) or 90° (abdomen).
  7. Inject slowly. Small 5s, medium 10s, large 15–20s.
  8. Withdraw, apply gentle pressure. Do not massage.
  9. Dispose immediately in sharps container.
  10. Rotate sites: Abdomen, thighs, love handles, glutes.

Large-Volume Injections (≥100 units):

Glutathione (200u) is the only large-volume injection. Use 3 mL syringe with 25G needle (IM glute), or split across 2 insulin syringes. Warm syringe in hand for 30 seconds before injecting.

Common Questions

Q: Why split Tesamorelin and Ipamorelin instead of using the blend?

Two reasons. First: Tesamorelin needs weekends off (pituitary GHRH receptor recovery), but Ipamorelin doesn't (no ghrelin receptor desensitization). Running Ipa on weekends gives better GH coverage and sleep quality. Second: if Tesamorelin's histamine reaction becomes an issue, you can drop Tesa and keep Ipa — with the blend you lose both.

Q: Can I combine injections?

Yes (same syringe): Tesamorelin + Ipamorelin (28u total), Pinealon + Selank (65u total), Wolverine (already blended). Never combine: GHK-Cu with anything (copper precipitates peptides). Tesa/Ipa with non-GH peptides (fasted state). IGF-1 LR3 (micro-dose — don't dilute). Glutathione (acidic).

Q: What if I miss a dose?

MOTS-c: Skip, resume next scheduled day. Tesamorelin/Ipamorelin: Skip — never double dose. IGF-1 LR3: Skip. DSIP: Skip. Pinealon: Resume next day; extend cycle by 1 day. All others: Skip, resume next day.

Q: Is 12 peptides too many?

This is a high-intensity, multi-goal protocol. Every peptide serves a distinct, non-redundant mechanism. The highest-risk component is IGF-1 LR3. If trimming: Glutathione and Selank are the lowest-consequence drops. The core seven — Tesamorelin, Ipamorelin, MOTS-c, Semax, Pinealon, DSIP, and GHK-Cu — deliver 85% of the protocol's total benefit across all 6 goals.
