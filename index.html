Format: Oral NMN (Nicotinamide Mononucleotide) capsules or sublingual powder. NOT injectable in this protocol — SubQ NAD+ is pH 2–3 (burns severely). NMN is the direct NAD+ precursor. Alternative: NR (Nicotinamide Riboside).

Dosing: 500–1,000 mg AM upon waking. Sublingual absorption improves bioavailability. No cycling needed — NAD+ declines with age, continuous restoration is the goal.

💡 Why oral NMN, not injectable: SubQ NAD+ is pH 2–3 — it burns intensely and requires 100–200 unit injections. Oral NMN achieves the same NAD+ elevation with zero pokes. Human trials (Igarashi 2022, Yoshino 2018) confirm NMN raises blood NAD+ significantly.

Common Questions

Q: When should I run Reta?

Only when you have a specific fat-loss target. The Greek Cheetah protocol uses Reta for 4–6 weeks during a 185→175 lb mini-cut. It's a focused intervention, not a lifestyle drug. Drop IGF-1 LR3 + MOTS-c during Reta, boost KPV + VIP + NMN. Plan the cut, run the cut, transition back to maintenance. NEVER run Reta continuously.

Q: Why VIP nasal and NAD+ oral instead of injectable?

VIP's half-life is ~2–5 minutes in blood. SubQ would require 4–6 injections/day. Nasal spray delivers VIP directly to the brain via olfactory bulb — where you want it most. NAD+ SubQ is pH 2–3 — it burns intensely and requires 100–200 unit injections. Oral NMN achieves the same NAD+ elevation (confirmed in human trials) with zero pain.

Q: Why drop Selank?

VIP nasal spray replaces Selank's anxiolytic function via a broader mechanism (neuroimmune regulation vs. GABA modulation) with zero injections. DSIP, Pinealon, and Ipamorelin already handle sleep from three complementary angles. Selank was layer 4 — nice to have, not need to have.

Q: Can I combine injections?

Yes: Tesamorelin + Ipamorelin (28u), Wolverine + KPV (36u). Never: GHK-Cu with anything (copper precipitates). Tesa/Ipa with non-GH peptides (fasted state). IGF-1 LR3 (micro-dose — don't dilute).

Q: What if I miss a dose?

MOTS-c: Skip, resume next scheduled day. Tesa/Ipa: Skip — never double dose. VIP nasal: Take when remembered. NMN: Skip if past 2 PM (energizing). Pinealon: Resume next day; extend cycle by 1 day. All others: Skip, resume next day.
