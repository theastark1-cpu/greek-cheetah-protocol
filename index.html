Injection Technique

  1. Wash hands thoroughly with soap and warm water.
  2. Wipe vial stopper with alcohol swab. Let dry completely.
  3. Draw air into syringe equal to dose volume. Inject air into vial.
  4. Draw dose slowly. Flick to dislodge bubbles, expel air.
  5. Wipe injection site with fresh alcohol swab. Let dry.
  6. Pinch skin fold (1–2 inches). Insert at 45° (lean areas) or 90° (abdomen).
  7. Inject slowly: Small volumes 5s, medium 10s, large (100u+) 15–20s.
  8. Withdraw needle, apply gentle pressure. Do not massage.
  9. Dispose immediately in sharps container. Never recap a used needle.
  10. Rotate sites: Abdomen, thighs, love handles, upper glutes. No same spot within 3 days.

Large-Volume Injections (≥100 units):

Glutathione (200u) is the only large-volume injection in the protocol. Use fattier sites (love handles, upper outer glute). Inject very slowly (20–30 seconds). Either use a 3 mL syringe with 25G needle (IM glute), or split across 2 insulin syringes at different sites. Warm the syringe in your hand for 30 seconds before injecting.

Common Questions

Q: Why is Tesa/Ipa #1 when it doesn't score highest in any single category?

Because it's good at everything. Tesa/Ipa scores 50+ in 4 of 6 categories — no other peptide does that. It's the foundation the rest of the stack is built on. The specialty peptides dominate their individual lanes but contribute nothing elsewhere.

Q: Can I combine injections to reduce the number of pokes?

Compatible in same syringe: Wolverine Stack (already blended), Tesa/Ipa (already blended). Can combine: Semax + Selank (similar pH). Never combine: Tesa/Ipa with anything else (fasted state, pH sensitivity). MOTS-c (full syringe already). IGF-1 LR3 (precise micro-dosing). Glutathione (acidic). Pinealon (gene expression peptide). DSIP (only 5 units — not worth combining).

Q: What happens if I miss a dose?

MOTS-c: Skip, resume next scheduled day. Semax: Skip. IGF-1 LR3: Skip, never double dose. Tesa/Ipa: Skip. Wolverine: Skip. DSIP: Skip. Selank: Take if within 2 hours; otherwise skip. Pinealon: Resume next day; extend cycle by 1 day. Glutathione: Take next day — flexible.

Q: How long until results?

DSIP: 1–3 nights. MOTS-c: 1–2 weeks. Semax: Same day. Tesa/Ipa: 8–12 weeks (noticeable); 26 weeks (measurable VAT). Glutathione/Pinealon: Cumulative — track biomarkers. GHK-Cu (hair/skin): 30–90 days. Wolverine: Days for pain; weeks-to-months for structural repair.

Q: Is 11 peptides too many?

This is a high-intensity, multi-goal protocol — not a starter stack. However, every peptide serves a distinct, non-redundant mechanism. The highest-risk component is IGF-1 LR3.
